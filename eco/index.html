Defenders of Earth

Remine India is a distinguished startup that focuses on recycling electronic waste and lithium batteries, which are some of the most hazardous materials that pose a significant environmental threat if not disposed of properly. With a strong commitment to sustainability and environmental protection, Remine India has developed innovative solutions that enable them to safely extract valuable materials from e-waste and batteries, thereby reducing their impact on the environment.

Save The Enviroment

Remine India has not only contributed to the preservation of the environment but has also promoted the growth of a circular economy by creating a sustainable supply chain for the recovery of valuable resources from waste. Their work has been recognized by various industry bodies, and they continue to set a benchmark for responsible e-waste management practices in the country.
